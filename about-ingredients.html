Deodorant Ingredients 💪

We've taken the utmost care to combine premium ingredients to create the perfect formula for your underarms so you can be sure that you'll smell amazing whatever life throws at you. For those with extra sensitive skin, try out our baking soda free range.

These are the natural ingredients which make up the base of our deodorants. We then add a combination of fragrance to create the Wild scents you love.

Tapioca Starch 🌱

An absorptive powder extracted from the roots of the cassava plant that absorbs moisture (aka your sweat) to keep your armpits dry.

Baking Soda

(Not in the baking soda free range) 🍰

Really effective at controlling odour and is added in minimal quantities to be gentle on the skin.

Sunflower Seed Wax 🌻

Rich in vitamin E to keep the skin moisturised.

Parfum 👃

All our deodorants are scented with a blend of fragrances to give you the Wild scents you love.

Cocoa Seed Butter 🍫

Slows the loss of water from the skin by forming a barrier on the skin's surface.

Tocopherol Acetate 🧑‍⚕️

A natural form of Vitamin E. It is an antioxidant so preserves the deodorant by preventing the degradation of the natural ingredients.

Caprylic/Capric Glycerides ⛸️

Used as an emollient so the deodorant glides smoothly across your underarms.

Stearyl Alcohol 🍹

A white, waxy solid that prevents the deodorant from separating into its oil and liquid components.

Triethyl Citrate 🍋

This ingredient keeps your refills looking and smelling fab!

Sunflower Seed Oil 🌻

An emollient added to make sure Wild glides on without tugging.

Coconut Oil 🥥

A really moisturising ingredient that has natural antibacterial and anti-fungal properties and smells great.

Shea Butter 🧈

A high quality butter with excellent moisturising properties (we're basically pampering your pits).

Magnesium Hydroxide 👃

Stops the growth of odour-causing bacteria on the surface of the skin without being absorbed into the skin to keep you smelling fantastically fresh.

Zinc Ricinoleate 👃

A fatty acid found in castor oil that can absorb odour.

Rice Starch

(Baking soda free range) 🌾

An absorptive powder to soak up sweat and oil.

Each deodorant is made from our base ingredients, plus a combination of fragrances.
